Our Mission

The National Environment Agency (NEA) is the leading public organisation responsible for ensureing a clean and sustainable environment for Singapore. We aim to improve and sustain a clean environment, promote Sustainability and resource efficiently, maintain high public health standards, provide timely and reliable meterorogical information, and encourage a vibrant hawker culture.

Safe, Healthy and Conducive Living Environment

We monitor and prevenr air and water pollution, and regulate hazardous substances and toxic industrial waste. To ensure a quality living environment, we use planning and siting of industries, and implement noise management measures. We also safeguard the public, radiation workers, and the environment against the harmful effects of radiation.
