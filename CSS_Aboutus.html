National Environment Agency (NEA)

Pioneering Environmental Sustainability in Singapore

Our Mission

The National Environment Agency (NEA) is the leading public organisation responsible for ensureing a clean and sustainable environment for Singapore. We aim to improve and sustain a clean environment, promote Sustainability and resource efficiently, maintain high public health standards, provide timely and reliable meterorogical information, and encourage a vibrant hawker culture.

Our Comprehensive Approach

NEA works closely with its partners and the community to develop environmental and public health initiatives and programmes. We are committed to motivating every individual to care for the environment as a way of life, building a liveable and sustainable Singapore for present and future generations.
